bugzilla-openid

Provides OpenID support for Bugzilla

Required Modules

bugzilla-openid needs Net::OpenID::Consumer in order to consume those tasty OpenID urls.

About

This plug-in was a dire request from the team behind the Synthetic Intellect Institute to provide a solid and unified means of logging into Bugzilla without having to use its cryptic system of logging in and registering.

Features

Bugzilla-OpenID allows the following:

  • Users can:

    • register with a valid OpenID Url.
    • login with said OpenID Url.
    • link accounts to OpenID Url.

  • Administration can:

    • require all users to attach a OpenID Url to accounts.
